Build your work portfolio
while you study

We offer a project-based learning ecosystem for our students. At Atria University, your learning experience is rooted in the real-world through 30+ projects that have the potential to create impact at scale. Engaging in these complex, day-to-day problems will enable you to learn contextually while gaining practical, on-field knowledge. Simply put, it’s not a classroom setup with lectures at the forefront. But one, where classes evolve into meaningful passion projects and classrooms extend to the world.

Studying at Atria University will shift your approach from that of a student to one of a versatile and resourceful professional. Through industry-driven projects, you will not only build your skills but also gain professional work experience, building your portfolio.

Discover your interest & craft your own degree

A core feature of our academic structure at Atria University is the freedom to pick a major and degree after 18 months. For the first two years, we want you to explore various domains and organically discover your inclinations and interests. At the end of this period, we believe you will be in a more qualified position to choose your career trajectory based on your experiences. With the flexibility to combine credits across courses, you can graduate with either a BBA, BDes, BSc, or a BTech depending upon your career ambitions.
